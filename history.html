History of the Homes of Cougars

Explore how Mahallah Ruqayyah got it inclusive and conducive living space thet differes from the others

Mahallah Ruqayyah, like other mahallahs, residential colleges IIUM, was established to promote the holisticdevelopment of students in line with Islamic principles and values. It aims to provide a secure and conducive living space for female students

Mahallah Ruqayyah were operational when it was formally launched in 2008 with only four block used. By 2009, nine blocks had beeen available fro use, bringing the total nnumber of blocks to fourteen(14) including the Central Facilities (CF)

The name "Ruqayyah" is inspired by the Islamic tradition. Ruqayyah bint Muhammad, also known as Umm Kulthum, was the daughter of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Naming the mahallah after her reflects the Islamic ethos of the university.
