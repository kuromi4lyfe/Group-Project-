ZOO NEGARA VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

In conjunction with IIUM's 40th Anniversary, the Mahallah Representative Committees (MRC) organized a voluntering program at Zoo Negara: Hearts for Paws.

The volunteering program offered a unique opportunity for participants to connect with incredible wildlife, contribute to conservation efforts, and create unforgettable memories. Together, the MRC nurtured a thriving environment for all our animal friends.

Date: 27th May 2023

Venue: Zoo Negara
